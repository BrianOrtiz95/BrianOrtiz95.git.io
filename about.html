ABOUT ME

I am a 28 year old Florida native with x amount of experience in fullstack development, but I did not start here. For six years I was an Infantryman in the United States Army, upon my departure from the service...

I became a Certified Personal Trainer and soon realized that profession was something that I could not sustain long term. Concluding my experimentation with various blue-collar trades, my fears only heightened. Time passed and as I grew older, I became frightened by the idea that the finish line was nearing and I had yet to find my "thing"...

The doubt was steadily creeping in, but I decided to take one more jump, a leap of faith you could say, into the tech industry. It is here that I have found passion and an endless outlet for creativity, a way of utilizing all of my strengths and skills while hushing the doubts that were once there.
